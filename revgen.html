This application will significantly streamline the Revenue Phase of your Republic of Rome games. Fill out the form below and click one of the Generate Revenue buttons. This will display the revenue that's generated for the State and each Faction.

Faction revenue is in the form of a "lump sum" rather than assigned to individual Senators. All revenue generated is to be added to what is already in play.

Most game situations are handled by this application. This includes legion & fleet maintenance, land bills, active wars, concessions, graft from Aegyptian and Sicilian Grain, provincial improvement, provincial spoils, provincial taxes, negative provincial spoils applied to the State rather than Faction, Pontifex Maximus's temple donations, and Provincial Wars. Barbarian Raids, Internal Disorder, provinces being attacked, and rebelling governors are not handled.

Clicking the Save Game button will download a file with the game's data. Reload previously saved games via the Load Game button.
